Privacy Policy for SupraBot

Last updated: April 20, 2026


1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data SupraBot ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and how it is stored. By using SupraBot, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.


2. Data We Collect

SupraBot collects the following data when you interact with the bot:

- Discord User ID: Your unique Discord identifier, used to create and maintain your player profile across servers.
- Discord Server (Guild) ID: The unique identifier of the servers where the bot is used, used to associate player data with the correct server.
- In-Game Data: All gameplay-related data generated through your use of the bot, including but not limited to: your car collection (garage), in-game currency balance (Carkera), upgrades purchased, trade history, car modifications, roll usage, and cooldown timers.
- Command Usage Logs: When you use a slash command, we log the server ID, your Discord username, and the command name for debugging and operational purposes. These logs are stored locally and rotated automatically.

We do NOT collect:

- Message content, beyond slash command interactions to perform the command (data is destroyed immediately after the command is performed)
- Direct messages
- Email addresses, IP addresses, or any personal information beyond your Discord User ID
- Voice or video data
- Data from users who have not interacted with the bot


3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

- Providing core gameplay: Managing your car collection, balance, rolls, trades, upgrades, and other game features.
- Per-server profiles: Maintaining separate player profiles for each server you play in.
- Leaderboards and rankings: Displaying server-wide rankings such as top cars and top garages.
- Debugging and maintenance: Command usage logs help us identify and fix issues with the bot.


4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored in a private PostgreSQL database hosted on infrastructure controlled by the bot developer.
- Log files are stored locally on the bot's host machine, automatically rotated at 5MB, with a maximum of 3 backup files retained.
- We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, but no system is 100% secure.


5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Your data is only accessible to the bot developer for maintenance and debugging purposes.


6. Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data is retained for as long as you use the bot.
- If you wish to have your data deleted, you can contact the bot developer through the support server or via Discord. We will remove your user profile and associated data upon request.
- When the bot is removed from a server, the server's data remains stored in case the bot is re-added. You may request deletion of server data by contacting the developer.


7. Children's Privacy

SupraBot does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 is using the bot, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.


8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated through the bot's support server or official listings. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to request data deletion, please reach out through:
