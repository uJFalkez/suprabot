## 🎮 What is SupraBot?

SupraBot is a Discord bot inspired by Mudae, but for **cars**. Every server gets its own economy where players compete to collect, tune, and trade the most valuable vehicles.

### ✨ Key Features

- 🎲 **Gacha Rolls** — Roll from a massive catalog of real-world cars with accurate specs and images.
- 🏎️ **Car Collection** — Build and manage your personal garage across multiple servers.
- 🔧 **Tuning System** — Install performance parts (exhaust, intake, brakes, and more) to boost your car's value.
- 💰 **Economy** — Earn Carkera (in-game currency) daily and through duplicate rolls. Buy upgrades, expand your garage, and invest in your collection.
- 🤝 **Trading** — Trade cars and Carkera with other players. Gift your friends their dream ride.
- 📊 **Leaderboards** — Compete for the top spot with server-wide rankings by car value and garage worth.
- 🛒 **Player Upgrades** — Unlock permanent bonuses like extra rolls, bigger garages, spare keys, and more.

## ⚡ Quick Start

1. **Invite** SupraBot to your server using the button above.
2. Use `/roll` to get your first car.
3. Use `/me` to check your profile and balance.
4. Use `/garage` to browse your collection.
5. Use `/upgrades` to power up your account.
6. Use `/help` for a full guide on any command.
